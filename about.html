Libra Fashion is a casual wear manufacturer and retailer aiming to become the leading clothing chain in Nepal. It makes sure that its products are reasonably priced and provides ‘the tools for fashion, so that everybody can express their own style’. It is continuously developing products of exceptional quality and grace to deliver an extensive line of fashion apparel.

Situated in the heart of the world's beautiful city, Kathmandu our fashion store will be able to provide various facilites that will be able to respect your choices and all your thoughts with the availability of the varied of goods with the varieties of goods ranging from old traditions competing with the modern world.

Libra Fashion was founded in 2001 A.D. aiming to provide grocery as well as various other services to the customers. The store went through the gradual growth to meet growing need of the market. 2010, it was reformed as superstore in order to provide more items including every handy things used in day to day life.

Suraj Thapa, the founder and CEO of Libra Fashion has further plans to expand the store and open its branches in different cities throughout the nation. The store also aims to promote the local fashionware as well as international brands, being a backbone to the local economy.

Since the establishment, quality products have been our top priority. Hence, we see forward for your support and collaboration in this task.
